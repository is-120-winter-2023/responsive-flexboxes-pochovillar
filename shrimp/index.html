Shrimp Ceviche

6 servings 500g cooked and peeled shrimp. 1 red onion, julienned. 1 ripe tomato, diced. ½ red bell pepper, diced. 1 avocado, diced. 1 mango, diced. 1 tablespoon of mustard. 4 tablespoons of ketchup. 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Juice of 4-5 limes. Juice of ½ orange. ½ cup of chopped cilantro. Salt, pepper

 

Mix all ingredients except for the orange juice and avocado (added at the end). Chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve. Before serving, adjust the seasoning, add the orange juice and avocado, and more salt if necessary.

Turn up the heat to high; stir in the zucchini, eggplant, peppers, and tomatoes, and season with salt and black pepper to taste. Uncover the pot, transfer to the oven, and bake, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender and lightly browned, about 1½ hours.

Remove the pot from the oven, then stir in the basil and parsley, and transfer the ratatouille to a wide bowl. Serve warm or at room temperature.
