Paella

Use a 25 cm diameter paella pan (6-8 servings) ½ kg of chicken thighs and/or seafood ½ cup of almonds Marinade for meat and seafood: 2 tablespoons of olive oil 1 garlic clove ½ teaspoon of dried oregano 1 tablespoon of smoked paprika ½ teaspoon of salt

 

Sofrito ½ cup of sofrito (can be purchased at the supermarket) To prepare the sofrito you will need: 1 ½ cup of diced onions, 2 minced garlic cloves (or frozen minced garlic), and 2 tablespoons of olive oil. 400g of diced canned or fresh tomatoes. Sauté until onions are soft. (Once cooled, blend or use directly without blending). You can freeze excess sofrito and use it the next time you make paella. Vegetables 1 ½ cups of vegetables (red bell pepper, green beans, mushrooms, artichokes, peas, red onion) (can use frozen) ¼ cup of chopped parsley, divided in half 1-2 lemons sliced Broth 2-3 saffron threads 2 ½ - 3 cups of chicken broth (can be made with boiled water and 3-4 chicken broth cubes) 1 tablespoon of soy sauce Rice 1 cup of bomba rice

Preparation: -Toast the almonds in the oven or in a pan until lightly browned. Grind in a mortar, food processor or chop finely with a knife. Only a couple of tablespoons are used, the rest can be stored in a ziplock bag in the freezer. -Mix the marinade in a bowl, divide the mixture to marinate the meat and seafood separately, can be in a bowl or a ziplock bag. -Cut the vegetables, you will need 1 ½ - 2 cups. -Toast the saffron for 10 seconds in the microwave. Grind and add to the 2 ½ cups of hot chicken broth along with the soy sauce. Keep warm until ready to use.

NOW IT'S TIME TO COOK!! -Heat 3-4 tablespoons of olive oil in the paella pan over medium heat. -Add the chicken, brown and cook for a few minutes. Remove from the pan and set aside on a plate. -If using shrimp, follow the same process as with the chicken. -Add more oil to the pan. Add the vegetables and brown for a few minutes. -Add the sofrito, stir. Add the rice, stir and then the broth. Stir making sure everything that is stuck to the bottom of the pan comes loose. -Distribute the vegetables over the rice, without stirring. -Let cook for 5 minutes. -Add the chicken, nestling it into the rice, without stirring. Cook for about 5 minutes. -Add the shrimp on top of the rice and sprinkle with the almonds. -Cook until the rice is done. If more liquid is needed, add more broth. -Once the rice is done, increase the heat for 1-2 minutes to form the socarrat (the almost burnt layer of rice at the bottom of the paella). -Turn off the heat, cover the paella with a lid, aluminum foil, or a kitchen towel for 10 minutes. -Sprinkle with parsley and decorate with lemon
